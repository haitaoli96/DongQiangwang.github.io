个人简介与研究兴趣

聚焦人兽共患隐孢子虫病及顶复门寄生虫的生活史与致病机制，主要研究微线体蛋白胞内运输/分泌、 宿主-寄生虫互作、分子诊断与卵囊活性定量检测，以及能量代谢与潜在药物靶点。

Profile & Research Interests

Focus on zoonotic cryptosporidiosis and apicomplexan parasites, including microneme protein trafficking/secretion, host–parasite interactions, molecular diagnostics and quantitative oocyst viability assays, parasite energy metabolism and novel drug targets.

主要学术贡献

  1. 在顶复门寄生虫中首次发现隐孢子虫微线体蛋白 CpTSP4 通过微管/驱动蛋白运输并分泌， 并揭示其与宿主细胞互作机制（mBio, 2024）。
  2. 在隐孢子虫领域首次建立 TaqMan qRT-PCR/qPCR 卵囊活性定量检测方法， 实现 C. parvum 与 C. hominis 卵囊存活力评估（Water Research, 2024）。

Major Academic Contributions

  1. First identified that Cryptosporidium micronemal protein CpTSP4 is transported and secreted via microtubule/kinesin-dependent trafficking and mediates host-cell interaction (mBio, 2024).
  2. Established a novel TaqMan qRT-PCR/qPCR assay enabling quantitative viability assessment of C. parvum and C. hominis oocysts (Water Research, 2024).
